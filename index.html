Explanation

Enter two numbers to see if any of the whole numbers between them satisfy the Eureka! rule.

The Eureka! rule is such that: "the sum of the numbers raised to the exponent of their respective positions gives you the sum of the numbers."

For example: 89 = 81 + 92
